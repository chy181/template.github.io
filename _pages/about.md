👩🏻‍💻 I'm a Professor and Ph.D. supervisor in the School of Data Science and Engineering at East China Normal University.

🔬 My research interests are in data management and analytics, machine learning, time series analytics, spatio-temporal data analytics, AI for Science, time series foundation models, AutoML, and multi-agent systems.

📫 I welcome undergraduate students, graduate students, Ph.D. students, and postdoctoral researchers to join the [Decision Intelligence Lab](https://decisionintelligence.github.io/).

# Selected Experience

## 👩🏻‍🔬 Professional Experience
Currently, I am a **Professor** in the School of Data Science and Engineering at East China Normal University. \
Before joining ECNU, I served as an **Associate Professor** in the Department of Computer Science at Aalborg University, Denmark.

My work focuses on data management and data analytics, with applications in intelligent transportation, digital energy, intelligent water-resource management, and data-driven decision making.

## 🎓 Education
I received my **Ph.D. from the University of Manchester**.

## 🧭 Research Directions
My main research directions include **Data Management and Analytics**, **Machine Learning**, **Time Series Analytics**, **Spatio-Temporal Data Analytics**, **AI for Science**, **Time Series Foundation Models**, **AutoML**, and **Multi-Agent Systems**.

## 📚 Publications and Service
I have published in leading international conferences and journals, including **Nature Communications**, **SIGMOD**, **VLDB**, **ICDE**, **IJCAI**, **AAAI**, **KDD**, **WWW**, **ICML**, **NeurIPS**, **ICLR**, **VLDB Journal**, and **IEEE TKDE**.

I also serve as a reviewer or program committee member for top journals and conferences, including **IEEE TKDE**, **IEEE TMC**, **ICML**, **NeurIPS**, **ICLR**, **IJCAI**, **AAAI**, **ICDE**, **KDD**, and **PVLDB**.

You can find my full publication list on [DBLP](https://dblp.org/pers/hd/g/Guo:Chenjuan) and [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.dk/citations?user=WMXNm88AAAAJ&hl=en).
